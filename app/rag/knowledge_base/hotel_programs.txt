TOPIC: Hotel Loyalty Programs Comparison
CATEGORY: Hotel Programs

MARRIOTT_BONVOY:
Program name: Marriott Bonvoy
Point value: ~0.8 cents per point
Network size: 8,000+ properties worldwide
Brands: Marriott, Sheraton, Westin, W Hotels, St. Regis, Ritz-Carlton,
        Courtyard, Fairfield, Renaissance, Autograph Collection
Transfer partners: Chase Ultimate Rewards (1:1), Amex MR (0.8:1),
                   Citi ThankYou (1:1), Capital One Miles (1:1)
Best redemption: Mid-tier properties (Sheraton, Westin) at off-peak times.
                 Category 1-3 properties offer best value.
Peak pricing: Major cities (NYC, London, Paris) require 50,000-100,000
              points per night at premium properties.
NYC example: Marriott Marquis Times Square ~40,000-60,000 points/night.

HILTON_HONORS:
Program name: Hilton Honors
Point value: ~0.5 cents per point
Network size: 7,000+ properties worldwide
Brands: Hilton, DoubleTree, Hampton Inn, Embassy Suites, Conrad,
        Waldorf Astoria, Curio Collection, Tapestry Collection
Transfer partners: Amex MR (1:2), Capital One Miles (1:2)
Best redemption: Standard rooms at mid-tier properties. Fifth night free
                 benefit for elite members on award bookings.
Peak pricing: Hilton requires more points than Marriott or Hyatt for
              comparable luxury properties.
NYC example: Hilton Midtown ~50,000-70,000 points/night.
Note: Hilton points are best obtained via Amex transfers (2:1 ratio)
      making Amex Gold the top card for Hilton redemptions.

WORLD_OF_HYATT:
Program name: World of Hyatt
Point value: ~1.5-2.5 cents per point (highest in industry)
Network size: 1,000+ properties (smaller but high quality)
Brands: Park Hyatt, Andaz, Grand Hyatt, Hyatt Regency, Hyatt Place,
        Hyatt House, Alila, Thompson Hotels, Tommie
Transfer partners: Chase Ultimate Rewards (1:1), Citi ThankYou (1:1),
                   Chase Freedom Unlimited (1:1)
Best redemption: Park Hyatt and Andaz properties offer exceptional
                 value. Category 1-4 properties are sweet spots.
Peak pricing: Category 1 = 5,000 pts/night. Category 8 = 40,000 pts/night.
NYC example: Park Hyatt New York ~25,000-35,000 points/night (Category 7).
Note: Hyatt offers the best cents-per-point value of any hotel program.
      Chase Sapphire Preferred is the premier card for Hyatt transfers.

IHG_ONE_REWARDS:
Program name: IHG One Rewards
Point value: ~0.5-0.7 cents per point
Network size: 6,000+ properties worldwide
Brands: InterContinental, Kimpton, Holiday Inn, Holiday Inn Express,
        Crowne Plaza, Hotel Indigo, voco, Regent
Transfer partners: Chase Ultimate Rewards (1:1), Capital One Miles (1:1)
Best redemption: Fourth night free on award bookings (huge value boost).
                 InterContinental properties offer best luxury value.
Peak pricing: InterContinental in NYC ~50,000-80,000 points/night.
NYC example: InterContinental Times Square ~60,000 points/night.
Note: The fourth night free makes IHG best for 4-night stays.
      Use Chase or Capital One cards for IHG transfers.
