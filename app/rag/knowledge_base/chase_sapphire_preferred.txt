CARD: Chase Sapphire Preferred
ISSUER: Chase
POINTS_CURRENCY: Chase Ultimate Rewards
VALUATION: 2.0 cents per point
ANNUAL_FEE: $95

HOTEL_PARTNERS:
- Marriott Bonvoy | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 5,000 points
- World of Hyatt | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 5,000 points
- IHG One Rewards | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 10,000 points

BEST_USE:
Chase Sapphire Preferred is best used for World of Hyatt transfers.
Hyatt offers the highest redemption value in the industry, often
yielding 2.5 to 5 cents per point at premium properties. The 1:1
transfer ratio means no points are lost in the transfer.

For Marriott Bonvoy, the 1:1 ratio is good but Marriott points are
generally worth less (0.8 cents each), so you need more hotel points
to cover a stay. Still a solid option for mid-tier Marriott properties.

IHG One Rewards transfers at 1:1 and IHG frequently runs 4th night
free promotions, making this card excellent for longer stays at
Holiday Inn, InterContinental, and Kimpton properties.

AVOID: Do not transfer Chase UR to hotel programs when you have less
than 5,000 points — you won't meet the minimum transfer threshold.

SWEET_SPOT: Park Hyatt properties. 5,000 Chase UR = 5,000 Hyatt points,
which can cover a night at a Category 1-2 Hyatt, delivering up to
5 cents per point in value.
