CARD: Citi Premier Card
ISSUER: Citi
POINTS_CURRENCY: Citi ThankYou Points
VALUATION: 1.8 cents per point
ANNUAL_FEE: $95

HOTEL_PARTNERS:
- Marriott Bonvoy | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 10,000 points
- World of Hyatt | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 10,000 points

BEST_USE:
Citi Premier offers strong 1:1 transfers to both Marriott and Hyatt,
making it a versatile hotel rewards card. The minimum transfer of
10,000 points is higher than Chase (5,000), so users need a larger
balance before transfers become available.

For Marriott Bonvoy, Citi Premier is competitive with Chase Sapphire
since both transfer at 1:1. However, Citi ThankYou Points are valued
slightly lower at 1.8 cents vs Chase's 2.0 cents, so Chase wins if
the user has both cards.

For World of Hyatt at 1:1, this is an excellent transfer — the same
quality as Chase Sapphire Preferred for Hyatt, making Citi Premier
a strong backup when Chase points are depleted.

AVOID: Do not use Citi Premier for hotel transfers if the user has
Chase Sapphire Preferred and the balance is above 5,000 points.
Chase offers better overall point valuation for the same programs.

SWEET_SPOT: Hyatt transfers when Chase balance is insufficient.
10,000 Citi TYP = 10,000 Hyatt points — a solid backup option
for Category 1-4 Hyatt properties.
