CARD: Capital One Venture X
ISSUER: Capital One
POINTS_CURRENCY: Capital One Miles
VALUATION: 1.85 cents per point
ANNUAL_FEE: $395

HOTEL_PARTNERS:
- Marriott Bonvoy | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 5,000 points
- Hilton Honors | Transfer ratio 1:2 | Minimum 5,000 points
- IHG One Rewards | Transfer ratio 1:1 | Minimum 10,000 points

BEST_USE:
Capital One Venture X is a premium travel card with broad hotel
transfer partnerships. Its Miles are valued at 1.85 cents each,
making it competitive with Chase and Amex for hotel redemptions.

For Hilton Honors, the 2:1 transfer ratio is the standout benefit —
5,000 Capital One Miles becomes 10,000 Hilton points, strong value
for Hilton properties at all tiers.

For Marriott Bonvoy at 1:1, Capital One is comparable to Chase
Sapphire but slightly lower value (1.85 vs 2.0 cents/pt). Choose
Chase first if both are available and balances are similar.

For IHG One Rewards at 1:1, Capital One is competitive. Combined
with IHG's fourth night free benefit, this card works well for
multi-night IHG stays.

AVOID: Do not use Capital One Miles for low-value hotel redemptions
under 1 cent per point — the $395 annual fee requires maximizing value.

SWEET_SPOT: High-balance holders (100,000+ miles) have the most
flexibility. 50,000 miles to Hilton Honors yields 100,000 Hilton
points — enough for multiple nights at mid-tier properties.
